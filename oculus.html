What the Oculus does

The Oculus is a very famous Virtual Reality Headset Company Owned by Facebook. The Oculus has many apps and games that you can play or see in 360° view. A Oculus VR lets you explore new worlds and attempt feats that seem unimaginable. And it has the potential to transform how we play, work, learn, communicate, and experience the world around us.

Why I like the Oculus and why you should get it too

I like the Oculus because it is so amazing that you can see things you have never seen before. For example: The Golden Gate Bridge, or The Twin Towers. You can also play super cool 360° games. The only downside is that a Oculus Quest 2 is $299|$399. But I think it is worth it. You also don't need to plug the headset into a wall outlet to stay on. You can charge it! 100% battery on a Oculus Quest 2 will give you 2 hours of playtime.
