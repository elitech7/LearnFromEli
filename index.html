All About Minecraft

Minecraft is a video game in which players create and break apart various kinds of blocks in three-dimensional worlds. The game's two main modes are Survival and Creative. In Survival, players must find their own building supplies and food. In Creative, players are given supplies and do not have to eat to survive.

All About Oculus

Oculus Quest is a virtual reality (VR) headset developed by Oculus, a division of Meta Platforms, Inc., released on May 21, 2019. Similar to its predecessor, Oculus Go, it is a standalone device that can run games and software wirelessly under an Android-based operating system.
